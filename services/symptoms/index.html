Symptoms Assistant

We like to think of Symptoms Assistant as our contribution to the community. This App allows you to record the most common symptoms (e.g. coughing, sneezing, fever) along with the most common vital signs (e.g. pulse, temperature, blood sugar) and track their progress over a period of time from one to 30 days.
